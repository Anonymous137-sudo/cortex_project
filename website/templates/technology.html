Technology

CryptEX is designed as a practical operator stack, not just a protocol concept.

Consensus, wallet recovery, sync behavior, RPC, chat, and packaging are all treated as part of the product surface.

Consensus

CryptEX uses SHA3-512 with full-width proof-of-work math, 512-bit chainwork, a network-specific genesis, and active longest-chain selection based on cumulative work.

Sync and storage

Headers-first sync, binary block storage, persisted chainstate snapshots, peer reputation, and block inventory exchange keep nodes catch-up capable without losing operator visibility.

Wallet model

BIP32 and BIP39 recovery, encrypted wallet files, multi-address support, balance separation between immature and spendable outputs, and secure chat identities are already in the stack.

Operational tooling

JSON-RPC, structured logs, config files, GUI-backed daemon management, miner consoles, and system datadirs are built for actual deployment rather than demos.
